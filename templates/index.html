Welcome to the Load Calculator

This application helps logistics professionals and individuals calculate and optimize the loading of cargo into containers or trucks.

How It Works

The Load Calculator allows you to:

  • Select the type of equipment (Container or Truck).
  • Input dimensions and characteristics of your cargo.
  • Automatically calculate the best way to load your cargo.
  • Review and export the results for your records or further analysis.
